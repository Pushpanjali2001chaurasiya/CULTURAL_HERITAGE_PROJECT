Siddhivinayak Temple, Mumbai

The Siddhivinayak Temple, located in the Prabhadevi area of Mumbai, is one of the most revered and visited temples in India.

The templeâ€™s main deity, Shree Siddhivinayak, is a black stone idol of Lord Ganesha with a unique feature: the trunk of the idol is tilted to the right, which is considered a rare and auspicious form1. The idol is also flanked by the goddesses Riddhi and Siddhi, who represent prosperity, fulfillment, and sanctity
