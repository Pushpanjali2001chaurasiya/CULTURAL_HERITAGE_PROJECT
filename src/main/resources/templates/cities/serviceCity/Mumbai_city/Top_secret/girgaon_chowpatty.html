It was one of the best expereince of Hindu rituals that I loved, normally greedy Pandas ruin everything.Girgaon Chowpatty, also known as Girgaum Chowpatty, is one of Mumbai’s most iconic beaches, located along the Queen’s Necklace adjoining Marine Drive in the Girgaon area. This public beach is a popular destination for both locals and tourists, offering a unique blend of cultural, recreational, and culinary experiences

The name “Chowpatty” is derived from the Marathi words “chau-pati,” meaning four channels or creeks. Historically, the beach has been a significant part of Mumbai’s social and cultural fabric. It is particularly famous for the annual Ganesh Visarjan celebrations, where thousands of devotees gather to immerse idols of Lord Ganesha in the Arabian Sea, marking the end of the Ganesh Chaturthi festival.
