Shri Mahalakshmi Temple, Mumbai

The Shri Mahalakshmi Temple in Mumbai is one of the city’s most revered and ancient temples, dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi, the goddess of wealth and prosperity. Located on Bhulabhai Desai Road in the Mahalaxmi area, the temple is a significant spiritual and cultural landmark.

The temple’s architecture is a blend of traditional Hindu styles, featuring intricate carvings and a serene ambiance that draws visitors from all over the world. The main sanctum houses the idols of the Tridevi goddesses: Mahalakshmi, Mahakali, and Mahasaraswati. These idols are adorned with gold bangles, pearl necklaces, and nose rings, symbolizing their divine and opulent nature.
