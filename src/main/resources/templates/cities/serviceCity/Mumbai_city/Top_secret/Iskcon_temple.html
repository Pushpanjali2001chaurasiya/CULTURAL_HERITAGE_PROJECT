ISKCON Temple, Mumbai

The ISKCON Temple in Mumbai, also known as the Sri Sri Radha Rasabihari Temple, is a prominent spiritual and cultural landmark located in the Juhu area. This temple is part of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON), a global spiritual movement founded by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada in 1966.

ISKCON Mumbai is not just a place of worship but also a vibrant community center. It offers a variety of spiritual and cultural programs aimed at promoting the teachings of Lord Krishna. These include daily kirtans (devotional singing), lectures on the Bhagavad-gita and Srimad Bhagavatam, and various festivals celebrating the pastimes of Lord Krishna.
